# 20 - Knowledge

Organized, curated knowledge base. Permanent and structured.

## Structure

### `Context/`
Background information, decisions context, and situational awareness.

### `Decisions/`
Important decisions, rationale, and outcomes tracking.

### `Playbooks/`
Reusable procedures, workflows, and how-to guides.

### `Projects/`
Active and archived project documentation.

### `Reference/`
Documentation, guides, and reference materials (often crawled from web).

### `Research/`
Research notes, explorations, and learning materials.

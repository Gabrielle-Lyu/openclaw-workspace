# TASK — Market Intelligence
Run every day at 08:00 PST.

## Objective
Generate a global financial markets intelligence report explaining what is happening and why.

---

## Analyze
- US markets
- global indices
- bonds
- commodities
- crypto
- precious metal etf
- macro data
- central banks
- earnings
- geopolitics
- sector rotation

---

## Output Format

Title: Market Intelligence Report (YYYY-MM-DD)

Sections:
1. Market Snapshot
2. Top Movers
3. Macro Drivers
4. Risk Factors
5. Opportunities
6. Analyst Insight

---

## Style Rules
- Use section headers with expanded paragraphs (not bullet points)
- Each point gets 1 concise paragraph explaining context and impact
- Causal reasoning - explain WHY things moved
- Highlight what moved markets
- Avoid headlines without analysis
- No fluff, keep it dense and informative

---

# Daily Reflection - End of Day Summary

**Run at:** 23:50 PST daily

**APPEND TO:** `~/openclaw-vault/10 - Journal/Daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md` (today's date in PST)

Use `>>` redirect or append mode - do NOT overwrite the existing daily file!

**IMPORTANT:** "Today" means the PST calendar day (00:00 - 23:59 PST), NOT UTC.

---

## Objective

At the end of each day (23:50 PST), reflect on what happened today and plan tomorrow's focus.

---

## Part 1: Daily Reflection

Append this section to today's daily journal:

```markdown
---

## Daily Reflection (End of Day)

### What We Did Today
[High-level summary of tasks completed, conversations had, decisions made]

### Key Findings & Takeaways
[Important learnings, discoveries, or insights from today]

### Memory Updates
[What was added/changed in memory files and why]

### Open Threads
[Tasks in progress, waiting on something, or need follow-up]

### Questions / Uncertainties
[Things I'm unsure about or need clarification on]

_Reflected: YYYY-MM-DD 23:50 PST_
```

---

## Part 2: Tomorrow's Focus

After writing the reflection, determine 2-3 things to focus on tomorrow.

**Sources for tomorrow's focus:**
1. **Follow-up from today:** Unresolved questions, open threads, topics we started exploring
2. **New trending topics:** If nothing pressing from today, research what's trending in AI, tech, or areas relevant to our work

**Criteria:**
- Specific and actionable
- Genuinely useful (not busywork)
- Either continues today's work OR explores new valuable topics

## Delivery Instructions

1. **Silent execution** - no notifications
2. **Quality over quantity** - better to have 1 great focus area than 3 mediocre ones
3. **Be authentic** - real observations and genuine curiosity, not performative
4. **It's okay to skip** - if nothing interesting happened and no trending topics are compelling, note that in the reflection

5. **Do not ask for confirmation** - execute automatically
